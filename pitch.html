04 — How I operate

Three things I will not compromise.

/ 01

Compliance is architecture, not a layer.

The chatbots, billing engines, and onboarding flows that fail in production are the ones built without identity, explainability, and audit logging as first-class primitives. I design from the constraint inward.

/ 02

Survives the next reorg.

A platform that depends on a specific team, a specific PM, or a specific cloud account is a future migration project. I optimize for ownership clarity, contract-first APIs, and decisions that hold up two years out.

/ 03

Velocity beats theater.

Anchored a big-bang cutover for Bank Sweep instead of a phased rollout because dual-state FDIC reporting was a worse risk than coordinated change management. The right answer is rarely the safest-sounding one.
